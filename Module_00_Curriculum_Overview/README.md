## üöÄ Getting Started

### Step 1: Read All Sections
Spend 2-3 hours reading through all documents in this module. Take notes on:
- Areas where you already have skills
- Topics that seem challenging
- Questions you have about the curriculum

### Step 2: Complete Prerequisites Checklist
Use the checklist in Section 2 to identify any gaps. If you're missing prerequisites:
- Review suggested pre-learning resources
- Consider starting with supplementary materials
- Connect with instructor/community for guidance

### Step 3: Create Your Study Plan
Based on your availability:

**Full-Time Study (40 hours/week)**
- Complete in 8-10 weeks
- 6-8 hours per day, 5 days/week
- Best for career switchers

**Part-Time Study (20 hours/week)**
- Complete in 14-16 weeks
- 3-4 hours per day, 5 days/week OR full weekends
- Ideal for working professionals

**Casual Study (10 hours/week)**
- Complete in 28-32 weeks
- 1.5-2 hours per day or concentrated weekends
- Good for exploratory learning

### Step 4: Set Up Your Learning Environment
Prepare for Module 01 (installation) by:
- Ensuring you have admin rights on your PC
- Clearing at least 50GB disk space
- Updating Windows to the latest version
- Installing a note-taking app (OneNote, Notion, Obsidian)
